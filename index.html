About UCO

A national platform built for
the people who build India.

The United Contractors Organisation (UCO) is a premier, pan-India professional body representing contractors, infrastructure developers, consultants and allied professionals.

With a strong network across India and international chapters in key countries, UCO works towards strengthening the construction ecosystem through collaboration, knowledge sharing, policy advocacy and professional excellence.

What UCO Offers

A comprehensive range of professional services.

Designed to empower your business and elevate industry standards — from representation to research to digital transformation.

Professional Representation

Advocating for the collective interests of contractors before government authorities, regulators and public institutions.

Technical & Legal Support

Guidance on contracts, procurement, dispute resolution, engineering practices and regulatory compliance.

Knowledge & Training

Conferences, seminars, workshops, certification programmes, technical sessions and leadership development.

Industry Research & Publications

Reports, newsletters, best practice guides, technical papers, market insights and policy recommendations.

Business Networking

Connecting contractors, consultants, suppliers, manufacturers, technology providers and financial institutions.

Government Liaison

Engaging with policymakers and public agencies for transparent and progressive industry policies.

Digital Transformation

Promoting adoption of modern technologies like BIM, AI, automation, smart construction and digital project management.

Supporting education, skill development, environment conservation, disaster relief and community welfare initiatives.

Membership & Global Network

Who can join UCO?

Ordinary Members

  • Civil, Electrical, Mechanical & Infrastructure Contractors
  • Government, Public Sector & Private Contractors
  • Registered Companies, LLPs & Partnership Firms
  • Specialised Contractors

Associate Members

  • Engineers, Architects, Project Management Consultants
  • Legal Professionals, Chartered Accountants
  • Technical Experts, Academicians

Corporate & Honorary Members

  • Corporate organisations and institutions
  • Industry leaders and eminent personalities
  • Significant contributors to the construction & infrastructure sector

Members enjoy networking opportunities, industry updates, technical support, capacity building, policy advocacy and exclusive member benefits.
